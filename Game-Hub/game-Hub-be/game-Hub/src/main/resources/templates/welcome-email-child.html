What is a Child Account?

A Child Account is designed to provide a safe and controlled environment for younger users. You can enjoy playing games, discovering new titles, and connecting with friends — all with additional safety measures enabled.

Parental Verification Required

Certain sensitive actions require the approval of your parent or guardian. Whenever you try to change specific settings, your parent will receive a verification code, which must be entered to confirm the change.

The verification code is required when attempting to:

  • Enable 16+ or 18+ content
  • Modify privacy or interaction settings:
    • Friend requests
    • Messages
    • Group invites
    • Play Together invites
  • Make any purchase or transaction

You are still free to change these settings at any time — but a parent must approve them first.

Switching to an Adult Account

Your account can be upgraded to an Adult Account in the future. After switching:

  • No parental verification will be needed
  • No approval emails will be sent to your parent
  • You will have full control over your account, settings, and purchases

This upgrade can be done at any time once eligibility requirements are met.
